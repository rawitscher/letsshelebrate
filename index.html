Get yo booties to NASHVILLE to celebrate our BRIDE. Your ticket to entry into our Airbnb is a negative COVID-19 test ;). Lucky duck for you, there's a clinic down the block that does instant testing. I hope you studied up on our sweet angel RBG queen Shelton because today is SHELTON DAY. Of course, it couldn't be Shelton day without a lingerie theme, so get crackin on those outfits ;). Saturday (rave cowgirl day)

We have a liiiiil surprise in the morning for ya. I can't say much, BUT there is a prettyyyyy realistic clue hidden in the links. We included the map in the links tab so you know exactly were we're going (or you can get to the next location if you are drunk and lost on Saturday). We'll have some fun group goodies to wear that day so any fun cowgal inspo is perfecto.

OK NOW IT'S RAVE TIME THO. So now please throw on your RAVE COWGIRL best for our party bus extravaganza! We have some fun surprises in store but here's what you NEED to know: it's a bus on the move so bring a purse to keep *ALL* your stuff in, and it's November, so bring a jacket :).
